SRI SAI

Bhavani Sri Sai Reddy is an architectural student specializing in sustainable and zero energy design.

A Bachelor of Architecture is being pursued (2021 - 2025) and a Diploma in Architecture Assistantship has been obtained from Polytechnic, Kakinada (2017 - 2020). Skills have been honed through practical experience as a 3D Interior Designer at Teksa Interio (2023 - 2024) and as an intern and assistant at Style Plus Structure Architects (2019 - 2020). Software such as AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Enscape was utilized to create detailed 3D models. Activities within NASA India were organized and promoted as a Unit Secretary. Close collaboration with architectural and design teams was undertaken.

Fluency in English and Telugu is maintained, and primary expression is achieved through tools like V-Ray, Photoshop, and Lumion. The career objective is to lead projects that prioritize eco-friendly practices and innovative design solutions, leveraging expertise in sustainable architecture to be a valuable asset to interdisciplinary teams.
